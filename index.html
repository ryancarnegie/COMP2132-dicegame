The Rules for Dice


  1. There are three rounds total:
  2. Each round, you and a computer opponent roll two dice.
  3. You earn a score based on the sum of the dice.
    • If either player rolls a 1, the players score for that round is 0.
    • If either player rolls a double of the same number (other than 1), your score for that round is multipled x2.
    • If the previous conditions are not met, your score for that round is the sum of two dice values.
  4. After three rounds, the player with the highest total score wins!
